About Us?

Body Buddies is a Brand new Personal Training, and Small Group Training studio based in Dublin 2. Our team consists of dedicated professionals exceeding 50 years’ experience in the fitness industry. We offer our members the highest level of expertise in personal training and nutrition guidance. In the comfort of top-class facilities, our coaching team will support you achieve your goals. Our main objective is to get the results you want. We provide the time necessary to build a strong foundation in technique and knowledge to enable you to achieve your personal goals. Our proven training system accompanied by a motivating and educating approach has helped members time and time again exceed their desired results. Without crash diets or quick fixes, we get guaranteed real results that will leave you feeling energised, healthy, and stronger than ever.

Private Personal Training


Welcome to your second home. You are unique and your training should be, too.
No matter your age or fitness level, we are invested in helping you reach your personal goals because we take your health, well….personally.
We don’t just jump in - we take the time to get to know you and understand where you are starting on your wellness journey, all before our head trainer who creates your custom workout program to put you on the path to success. Whether you’re training in-studio or training virtually, we’re in this together.

Facilities
